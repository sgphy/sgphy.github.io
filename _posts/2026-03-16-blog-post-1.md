Since I was a teenager, my mom tried to inculcate a habit of writing daily journals. I was inconsistent and mostly wrote impulsively to probably dump emotions.
Happiness, heartbreaks or anger. Once, I even tried to write a detective story - pretty much mimicking Feluda, which I never finished. Over the last two years, I have gradually gone back more towards writing down things in my diary. It felt fulfilling, particularly writing in Bangla with a fountain pen.

I had the idea of writing blogs since I was in university, when blogging became trendy. I wanted to be cool too but at that age trying make a place for yourself, life takes you with the tide (call it an excuse!). Nevertheless, it has not happened until now evidently.
I graduated with my doctorate 2 years back and have tried to get back to the things I could not invest much time before, such as reading. And with time I also realised, that I should document some parts of my thoughts publicly. Hence, this blog.

I will be mostly writing about socio-politics, culture and science. Thank you for reading and welcome to my blog.
