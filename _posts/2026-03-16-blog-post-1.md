I have decided to not have any kids or at the least not bring a new life into this world. Of the handful of persons we get to know during our lives, we are mostly remembered by our closest ones who we leave behind, and some bunch of acquaintances
who are kind enough to remember for some reason or the other. This is the person that you have been around these people. Besides, you are largely remembered by the work you leave behind, although, this does depend on your profession (in some more than the others). 
And then there is a third way. You procreate and leave a legacy behind. When I say legacy I mean a part of your personality/attributes, not a business empire.

I believe when life gives you choices, you make a decision and every decision has consequences. One should be bold enough to deal with the consequences. As I have decided to not have kids (my decision indeed), I will try to leave my thoughts through blog posts over here. So as long as Github lives, I will probably live.
